TITLE (≤30 characters)
Petite Jérusalem — Torah

SHORT DESCRIPTION (≤80 characters)
Study and share Torah together: Tehilim, Talmud, Tanakh. Free, no ads.

LONG DESCRIPTION (≤4000 characters)
Petite Jérusalem is a free app to study and share Torah, on your own or together with others.

📚 Full library: Tehilim, Mishnah, Talmud (Gemara) and Tanakh, with a reader built for Hebrew text and its translation.

🤝 Shared reading: create a session to split study between several people — finish the Chass (Talmud) together by sharing out the tractates, read all 150 Tehilim as a group for a refuah shelemah or in memory of a loved one — and track progress all the way to the siyum.

📖 Offline reading: download texts to read them without a connection, on a plane or off the grid.

⏰ Daily reading reminder: a notification at the time you choose, so you never miss your daily reading.

🎧 Chiourim: a selection of audio lessons (shiurim) to listen to.

Petite Jérusalem is completely free, ad-free, and available in French, English and Hebrew. An account is useful to keep your preferences and progress, but is not required to read.
